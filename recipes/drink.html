Drinks

Description

Banana milk is a beverage made from blended bananas and water. It's a dairy-free, nut-free option you can use just like other types of milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 4 to 5 ice cubes(optional)

Steps

  1. Combine banana, milk, water, maple syrup and vanilla in the blender cup. Blend until smooth.
  2. Place ice cubes in glass (if using). Pour banana milk on top.
